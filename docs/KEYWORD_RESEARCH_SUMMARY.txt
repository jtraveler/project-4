QUESTIONS & ANSWERS
================================================================================

Q: How long until I see organic traffic?
A: Week 3-4 typically shows first organic visits. Page 1 rankings take 8-12
   weeks for Tier 1 keywords, 3-4 weeks for Tier 2 long-tail.

Q: Do I need to hire a writer?
A: No, but it helps. One person can write 2-3 quality posts/week. At 50 posts
   needed, that's 4-5 months of part-time work (doable solo).

Q: What's the most important keyword to start with?
A: "Cyberpunk Midjourney prompts" - high conversion, low competition, fast
   ranking. Gets you initial traffic momentum.

Q: Should I focus on Tier 1 or Tier 2 keywords first?
A: Start with Tier 2 long-tail keywords to build momentum fast, then use that
   authority to rank for Tier 1 keywords. Opposite of traditional SEO.

Q: How much will this cost?
A: Nothing if you write yourself. Optional: $99/year (Keywords Everywhere
   extension) + $99+/month (SEMrush/Ahrefs for competitive analysis).

Q: When should I start link building?
A: Month 3+, after you have 20-30 quality articles. Link building before then
   wastes outreach efforts.

Q: What's the biggest risk?
A: Not publishing enough content. The long-tail strategy requires 50+ posts.
   If you only publish 10, you won't see the traffic multiplication effect.
