Content Policy

Pre-launch placeholder. PromptFinder is currently in development. This page will be expanded with a full content policy before public launch.

Basic rules

While we finalize our full content policy, the following rules apply:

  • No content involving minors in any sexual context. Zero tolerance.
  • No graphic violence, gore, or content depicting torture.
  • No hate speech, harassment, or content targeting individuals or groups.
  • No content that infringes others' copyrights or trademarks.
  • No content depicting real, identifiable people without their consent (especially in suggestive or compromising contexts).

AI provider content policies

Each AI provider PromptFinder uses (Nano Banana 2, GPT Image, Grok Imagine, Flux) has its own content policies. PromptFinder enforces these provider policies on submissions made through our platform.

If your prompt is blocked, the AI provider's content policy flagged it. Edit the prompt and try again, or try a different model — Flux models are generally more permissive for artistic content than Nano Banana 2 or GPT Image.

Reporting issues
