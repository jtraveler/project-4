## ðŸŽ“ SEO Fundamentals Checklist

### Every Blog Post Must Have:

**Technical SEO (10 minutes per post)**
- [ ] Title (50-60 characters, includes primary keyword)
- [ ] Meta description (155-160 chars, includes keyword + CTA)
- [ ] H1 tag (includes keyword variant, only 1 per page)
- [ ] URL slug (keyword-based, lowercase, hyphens)
- [ ] Internal links (8-10 links to related pages)

**Content SEO (Writing/editing)**
- [ ] Primary keyword in first 100 words (3-4 mentions total)
- [ ] Related keywords naturally throughout (LSI keywords)
- [ ] Keyword density: 0.5-1.5% (avoid stuffing)
- [ ] H2-H6 headers organized logically
- [ ] Image alt text includes keywords
- [ ] Natural conclusion with CTA

**Schema Markup (5-10 minutes)**
- [ ] Article or BlogPosting schema
- [ ] FAQPage schema (if applicable)
- [ ] Breadcrumb schema
- [ ] Author/Creator markup

**Internal Linking Strategy (5 minutes)**
- [ ] Link to 3-5 category pages
- [ ] Link to 2-3 related blog posts
- [ ] Link to tag pages (3-5 tags)
- [ ] Natural anchor text (vary it up)

**Mobile & Performance**
- [ ] Responsive design (test on mobile)
- [ ] Images optimized (compressed)
- [ ] Load time <3 seconds (Google PageSpeed)
- [ ] Readable font size (16px+ body)

   - Identifies high-performing content

   - See search trends over time
   - Identify seasonal keywords
   - Find related keywords
   - Use before writing: 5 minutes per article

4. **Google Keyword Planner** (free, requires Google Ads account)

   - Competition levels
   - CPC data
   - Setup: 10 minutes

## â“ FAQ

### Q: How long until I see organic traffic?
**A:**
- Week 2-3: First rankings appearing (page 3-5)
- Week 4-6: Page 2 rankings
- Week 8-12: Page 1 rankings (top 10)
- First organic traffic usually around week 3-4

### Q: Do I need paid ads to rank?
**A:** No. Paid ads and organic SEO are completely separate. However, paid ads can help you test content ideas quickly (which paid searches convert best) before investing time in organic content.

### Q: How much content do I really need?
**A:**
- Minimum: 10-15 blog posts to establish presence
- Optimal: 50+ blog posts to dominate your niche
- Ideal: 100+ blog posts for complete market dominance
- Law of diminishing returns kicks in after 100+ posts (growth slows)

### Q: Which keywords should I target first?
**A:** Start with Tier 2 long-tail keywords (Cyberpunk Midjourney, Fantasy DALL-E). They:
- Rank faster (less competition)
- Convert better (specific intent)
- Get you initial traffic (momentum)

THEN leverage that authority to rank for Tier 1 keywords.

### Q: Should I create YouTube videos?
**A:** Yes, but later. Priority:
1. Blog content (easiest, fastest ROI)
2. SEO optimization of existing content
3. Link building
4. YouTube content (month 6+)

### Q: How do I get backlinks?
**A:** After month 3, when you have 30+ quality articles:
1. Email outreach to related blogs
2. Guest posts on AI art blogs
3. Resource roundups ("50 AI Art Blogs")
4. Interviews with creators
5. Naturally earned (best articles attract links)

### Q: What about social media?
**A:** Social media is secondary to organic SEO for PromptFinder. But they work together:
- Blog drives organic traffic
- Social drives brand awareness
- Both drive premium conversions
- Start with blog, add social in month 4-6
