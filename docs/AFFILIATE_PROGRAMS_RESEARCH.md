## Legal & Compliance Considerations

### Affiliate Disclosure Requirements

**FTC Requirements (USA):**
- Clearly disclose affiliate relationships
- Use language like: "We earn commissions from some links at no cost to you"
- Disclosure must be clear and conspicuous
- Place near or with affiliate link

**GDPR Compliance (EU):**
- Comply with affiliate program terms (usually handles this)
- Impact.com ensures GDPR compliance
- No special disclosure needed beyond FTC

**Recommended Disclosure Statement:**
```
PromptFinder is a participant in affiliate programs with Leonardo AI,
Adobe, Pika, and other AI tool providers. When you click on a "Created
with [Tool]" badge or affiliate link, we may earn a commission at no
additional cost to you. These commissions help support our platform
development.
```

### Brand Guidelines Compliance

**Leonardo AI:**
- Don't use "Leonardo" in paid ads
- Use only approved badge/logo
- Don't misrepresent partnership

**Adobe:**
- Flexible, no special restrictions mentioned
- Follow standard affiliate best practices

**Pika:**
- No paid ads/social campaigns
- Standard affiliate policies apply

### Trust & Transparency

**Best Practices:**
1. Never hide affiliate relationships
2. Only recommend tools you believe in
3. Focus on user value, not commission
4. Disclose prominently and early
5. Don't mislead users into clicking affiliate links
6. Balance affiliate links with genuine recommendations
